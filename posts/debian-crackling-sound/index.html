Sometimes i hear pretty bad distortions, lags and cracks while listening to spotify or playing games. The following settings helped me in my case.

$ sudo nano /etc/pulse/daemon.conf

# search for ; default-fragment-size-msec
# remove the ; and set its value to something below 50
# for its working with 5
# save, quit and restart pulseaudio

pulseaudio -k;pulseaudio --start
